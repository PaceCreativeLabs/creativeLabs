Student Developers.

Seidenberg Creative Labs takes pride in the fact that we are run by students. We bring skilled graduate and undergraduate students together to work on professional products. Starting in their Sophomore year, our developers are managed by experienced seniors and alumns, who teach them the tricks of the trade.

Through Creative Labs, students gain real world experience which prepares them for internships and full-time jobs after graduation. Some have even moved on to form their own startups!

Our Skills

  • Research

  • Consultancy
